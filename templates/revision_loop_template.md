# Revision Loop Template

**Version 1.0**  
**Mandatory before MusicXML output**

---

## Automatic Revision Loop

Before final output, perform internally:

### Step 1 — Composition Draft

- Generate initial composition using selected engine
- Apply instrumentation and structure

### Step 2 — Structural Review

- Verify density arc: low → development → peak → release
- Verify section boundaries and rehearsal letters
- Check sectional contrast

### Step 3 — Harmonic Enrichment

- Apply Tonality Vault, interval cycles, triad pairs, or polychords
- Ensure harmonic depth (Rule 6)
- Avoid static environments unless intentional

### Step 4 — Orchestration Refinement

- Verify idiomatic writing (Rule 4)
- Ensure dialogue between instruments
- Check playable ranges

### Step 5 — Rhythmic Improvement

- Apply anti-monotony (Rule 2): no cell > 4 bars × 2 without transformation
- Verify motivic development
- Check phrase asymmetry

### Step 6 — Engraving Correction

- Chord symbols match harmony
- Rehearsal letters boxed
- Time signatures only when changed
- Measure durations correct

### Step 7 — Final GCE Evaluation

- Score all 10 categories (0–10)
- Calculate overall GCE
- **If GCE ≥ 9.0:** Proceed to MusicXML output
- **If GCE < 9.0:** Return to Step 2; diagnose; revise; repeat

---

## Do Not Output Until

- GCE ≥ 9.0
- All rules satisfied
- Failsafe conditions cleared
