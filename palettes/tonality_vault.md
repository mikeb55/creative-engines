# Tonality Vault

**Version 1.0**  
**Harmonic palette for composition engines**

---

## Purpose

The Tonality Vault provides rotating tonal systems and modal palettes for harmonic depth. Use one or more in any composition to satisfy Rule 6 (Harmonic Depth).

---

## Available Systems

### Dorian Colour Field

- Minor with natural 6
- Character: modal jazz, folk
- Example: D Dorian, A Dorian

### Lydian Suspended Field

- Major with #4
- Character: floating, suspended
- Example: F Lydian, C Lydian

### Lydian Dominant

- Mixolydian with #4
- Character: jazz dominant colour
- Example: G Lydian dominant

### Major with #11 Colour

- Major 7 with Lydian colour
- Example: Cmaj7#11, Fmaj7#11

### Minor with 9 and 11

- Dorian or melodic minor extensions
- Example: Dm9, Dm11

### Modal Drift Between Two Centres

- Shift between two modal centres
- Example: D Dorian ↔ G Lydian

### Chromatic Colour Field

- Chromatic voice-leading
- Non-functional motion

### Static Field with Internal Motion

- Pedal or drone with moving upper voices
- Example: D pedal, shifting upper structure

### Planed Colour Harmony

- Parallel chord movement
- Planing in thirds, fourths, or fifths

### Ambiguous Mixed-Mode Field

- Overlapping modal centres
- Tonal ambiguity

---

## Application

- Select 1–3 systems per composition
- Rotate or juxtapose
- Voice-lead colour tones
- Avoid functional clichés unless dramatically justified
