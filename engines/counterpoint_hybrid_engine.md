# Counterpoint Hybrid Engine

## Harmonic Language

Multiple tonal centres; modal interchange; chromatic colour; upper-structure; planed harmony. Use Tonality Vault, interval cycles, polychords. Voice-led; avoid block chords.

## Rhythmic Philosophy

Independent rhythmic profiles per voice. Contrary motion. Delayed entries and exits. Phrase overlap. Stepwise or cell-based logic.

## Orchestration Behavior

*Adapt to any ensemble:* Primary (independent line, drives texture); secondary (equal contrapuntal partners); inner (crucial inner-motion); bass (anchor, contrary-motion). Each voice has melodic independence.

## Motif Development Approach

Motifs migrate between voices. Interval expansion, inversion, fragmentation. Every voice justifies itself through harmonic colour, rhythmic tension, register balance, or motivic development.

## Structural Tendencies

Through-composed arcs; theme and development; texture-evolution form. Density arc: low → development → peak → release.
