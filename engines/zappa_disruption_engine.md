# Zappa Disruption Engine

**Secondary engine only — never primary.**

## Harmonic Language

Sharp dissonance; polychord collisions; sudden harmonic shifts. Use polychords, interval cycles. Used only in selected sections; does not define piece harmony.

## Rhythmic Philosophy

Sudden attacks; coordinated ensemble hits; rhythmic interruption; fractured continuity. Limited duration (few bars to one section).

## Orchestration Behavior

*Adapt to any ensemble:* All voices may participate in coordinated hits. Role: accent, fracture, collision — not sustained character. Must not dominate.

## Motif Development Approach

Disruption contrasts primary engine material. No sustained motif development; function is punctuation and contrast.

## Structural Tendencies

Placement: development, climax, or contrasting episode. Density spike at disruption. Primary engine structure remains intact.
