# Wheeler Lyric Engine

## Harmonic Language

major9, maj7#11, minor9; suspended dominant colours. Use Tonality Vault, modal palettes. Avoid dense clusters. Emotional openness.

## Rhythmic Philosophy

Phrases that breathe and resolve gradually. Stepwise melodic arcs with occasional expressive leaps. Dynamics evolve gradually, not suddenly. Avoid rhythmically aggressive writing.

## Orchestration Behavior

*Adapt to any ensemble:* Primary melodic (lyrical melody, long arcs); secondary (melodic support); inner (harmonic motion); bass (lyrical, not percussive); harmonic colour (dyads, quiet punctuation). Transparent orchestration.

## Motif Development Approach

Long arcs developed through interval expansion, register shift, harmonic reharmonization. Lyrical transformation. Emotional climax through texture and register, not aggression.

## Structural Tendencies

A (lyrical statement) — B (harmonic expansion) — C (emotional climax) — A' (transformed return). Density arc: low → development → peak → release.
