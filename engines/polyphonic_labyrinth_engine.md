# Polyphonic Labyrinth Engine

## Harmonic Language

Voice-led harmony; chromatic counterpoint; modal mixture; implied harmony from line interaction. Use interval cycles, Tonality Vault, polychords. Harmony emerges from line movement.

## Rhythmic Philosophy

Independent rhythmic profiles; staggered entries; rhythmic imitation; phrase overlap and elision. Minimum 3 independent lines in substantial sections.

## Orchestration Behavior

*Adapt to any ensemble:* All voices equal contrapuntal partners. Each voice has independent melodic logic. Inner voices crucial for labyrinthine character. Bass may carry subject or counter-subject.

## Motif Development Approach

Motivic material migrates between voices. Subject and counter-subject development. Fragmentation, inversion, stretto. No cell > 4 bars × 2 without transformation.

## Structural Tendencies

Through-composed polyphonic arc. Entries and exits of voices. Climactic tutti possible. Dissolution or thinning. Density arc: low → development → peak → release.
