# GCE — Swing Authority

**Activation:** When the idiom calls for swing, groove, or jazz-pop phrasing.

---

## Scoring Lens

When relevant, evaluate whether rhythmic placement, syncopation, phrasing, backbeat awareness, anticipations, and rests create convincing stylistic motion rather than square notation.

---

## Scoring Guidance

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **7** | Technically acceptable but somewhat square |
| **8** | Stylistically convincing in places |
| **9** | Strong swing authority; feels played |
| **10** | Inevitable, identity-level rhythmic feel |

---

## What to Assess

- Mixed beat placement vs. grid-lock
- Syncopation and displacement
- Anticipations into barlines
- Rests and rhythmic breathing
- Whether notation suggests a performed feel

---

## Implementation

- Evaluative lens only; activates when idiom is relevant.
- Does not apply to non-swing contexts (e.g. rubato ballad, through-composed).
