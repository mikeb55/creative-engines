# GCE — Countermelody / Line Interaction

**Activation:** When 2 or more meaningful lines are present.

---

## Scoring Lens

When relevant, evaluate whether secondary lines are purposeful, well placed, independent, complementary, and musically conversational.

---

## Scoring Guidance

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **7** | Present but generic or crowded |
| **8** | Clear and supportive |
| **9** | Conversational and well integrated |
| **10** | Indispensable to the piece |

---

## What to Assess

- Role clarity (riff, pad, hit, fill, call-response, guide-tone, counterpoint)
- Placement (gaps, held notes, phrase endings, transitions)
- Line independence (register, density, motion)
- Support vs. clutter
- Whether lines sound like they are listening to each other

---

## Implementation

- Evaluative lens only; activates when multiple lines exist.
- Preserves musical intelligence; not a rigid formula.
