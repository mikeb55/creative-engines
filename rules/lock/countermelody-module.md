# LOCK — Countermelody / Secondary-Line Module

**Activation:** Only when 2 or more meaningful lines are present.

---

## Purpose

Ensure secondary lines have clear roles and support rather than clutter. Prioritise clarity, contrast, and conversation over constant simultaneous activity.

---

## Role Taxonomy

Each secondary line must have a clear function. Roles may include:

- **Riff** — repeated figure with identity
- **Pad** — sustained support
- **Hit** — punctual punctuation
- **Fill** — transitional or phrase-ending material
- **Call-response** — answer to another line
- **Guide-tone support** — 3rds, 7ths, harmonic implication
- **Counterpoint** — independent melodic contour

---

## Placement

Prefer secondary lines in:
- Gaps in the melody
- Held notes
- Phrase endings
- Sectional transitions

Avoid prolonged dual-density unless intentionally justified.

---

## Implementation

- Activates only when 2+ active lines exist.
- Preserves flexibility; does not impose hard numeric quotas.
- Supports jazz and jazz-pop; only active when relevant.
