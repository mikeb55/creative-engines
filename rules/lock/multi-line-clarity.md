# LOCK — Multi-Line Clarity Gate

**Activation:** When 2 or more lines are active simultaneously.

---

## Purpose

Preserve line independence. Avoid muddy overlap, unnecessary voice crossing, and prolonged moments where both lines are rhythmically dense unless intentionally justified.

---

## Core Guidance

- **Register separation** — Use distinct registers to clarify line identity
- **Density control** — When one line is active, the other may rest or punctuate
- **Contrasting motion** — Independent contours reinforce clarity
- **Avoid:** Prolonged dual-density writing unless musically justified
- **Avoid:** Unnecessary voice crossing and mud

---

## Gate

Before output, confirm:
- Lines are distinguishable by register, density, or motion
- No extended passages where both parts are dense and competing
- Each line has audible space to breathe

---

## Implementation

- Evaluative lens, not universal mandate.
- Activates only when multiple meaningful lines are present.
