# Engine Rotation Examples

**Version 1.0**  
**For album and program planning**

---

## Purpose

To ensure variety across an album or program, rotate engines and avoid consecutive pieces with the same character.

---

## 9-Track Album Example

| Track | Engine | Character |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| 1 | Frisell Atmosphere | Spacious opener |
| 2 | Shorter Narrative | Motif-driven |
| 3 | Scofield–Holland | Groove |
| 4 | Counterpoint Hybrid | Contrapuntal |
| 5 | Wheeler Lyric | Lyrical ballad |
| 6 | Stravinsky Pulse | Rhythmic energy |
| 7 | Bartók Night | Nocturnal contrast |
| 8 | Polyphonic Labyrinth | Dense development |
| 9 | Frisell Atmosphere | Spacious close |

---

## Rotation Guidelines

- **No two consecutive pieces** use the same primary engine
- **Zappa secondary** in ≤ 2 pieces per album
- **Alternate tempo** — slow / medium / fast
- **Alternate texture** — sparse / dense
- **Balance** — roughly equal representation across engines
